Let's cook some Lasagna!

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian pasta dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with a rich and savory filling. The traditional filling typically consists of alternating layers of pasta, Bolognese sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and grated cheese, often including mozzarella and Parmesan.

Ingredients

(8 servings)

  • 500 grams of pasta sheets
  • 1 liter of Béchamel sauce
  • 400 grams of minced beaf
  • 50 grams of onions
  • 50 grams of carrots
  • 300 grams of tomato sauce>
  • 100 grams of parmesan cheese
  • Oil, salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the bolognese sauce
    In a pan, pour some oil and, when it becomes hot, throw the veggies in; this is what we call a "soffritto". Cook for 3-4 minutes then proceed to add the minced beef. Let the meat brown and then add the tomato sauce. Cook for about 2 hours while making sure to stir everything up so that it doesn't stick to the bottom of the pan.
  2. Assemble the dish
    Take a large baking tray, grease the bottom and add a thin layer of Béchamel sauce and one of Bolognese sauce; proceed then to place the first sheets; When the first layer is completed, repeat the same process all over again until you have used up all the ingredients. Sprinkle the top layer with parmesan cheese.
  3. Baking
    Proceed to bake in a preheated static oven at 170° C for 40 minutes
  4. Enjoy
